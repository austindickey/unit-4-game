Crystal Collector

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your current score.

You win the game by matching your current score to the random number; you lose the game if your current score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you, until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.

Good luck!
